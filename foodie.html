The Foodie

There are dozens of great restaurants in Richmond. To get the most out of your visit, you should focus on the many great restaurants on Cary Street and West Main. Below are some of the recommendations we have for your meals and your sweet tooth!

8:30-10:00 a.m. Breakfast at the Daily in Carytown.
10:00-12:00 p.m. Explore Carytown and its many restaurants and dessert shops. Stop by Dixie Donuts for a treat.
12:30-2:00 p.m. Lunch at Can Can Brasserie, a French restaurant in the heart of Carytown
2:30-4:00 p.m. Stop by Shyndigz in the Fan near VCU for some wine, cake, and other sweets!
5:00-7:00 p.m. End your day with dinner at Heritage near downtown Richmond.
