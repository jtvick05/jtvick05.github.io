The Unmedicated is a graphic novel to articulate mental health issues and recovery. Copies can be purchased using bitcoins for hard copies. Please allow 6 weeks for delivery.

The story opens with a Certified Peer Specialist stumbling upon a drunk prostitute on the edge. Mental health recovery issues are discussed over the course of an evening that fades into morning.

All writing, photos & art were created by Scott Haugh, Director of Art-Lottery, a sales tool for artists.
