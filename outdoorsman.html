The Outdoorsman/woman

To enjoy a day outdoors in Richmond, you will first need energy. You will start your day at Weezie's Kitchen for breakfast. From there, you will need to get snacks and lunch for a day on the James River. We suggest Ellwood Thompson's, a local grocery store. Spend the day kayaking on the James River and end your day with a hike through the gardens of Maymont Park where you will get to see some of the wildlife of Virginia.

9:00-10:30 a.m. Breakfast in Carytown at Weezie's Kitchen
10:30-11:30 a.m. Stop by Ellwood Thompson's, a locally-owned and sourced grocery store, to pick-up some healthy snacks and lunch items for your trip on the James River.
11:30-4:00 p.m. Head to Riversde Outfitters for your guided tour or kayak/paddleboard/raft rental.
4:00-7:00 p.m. Go to Maymont Park and enjoy the gardens and Virginia wildlife at this historic riverside estate.
7:00-9:00 p.m. Dinner at Alamo BBQ. A great meal to end a day of outdoor activities!
