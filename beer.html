The Beer Enthusiast

Remember to be safe as you visit each brewery. Uber has gained popularity in Richmond and would be a great option for getting around. That said, you will get a great taste of Richmond beer by following this itinerary!

10:00-11:30 a.m. Brunch in Carytown at Weezie's Kitchen
12:00-1:30 p.m. Start at Strangeway's Brewery. The name says it all, they create unique beer flavors with unique ingredients.
1:30-2:30 p.m. Grab some lunch from one of Richmond's many Food Trucks. This will help keep you from drinking on an empty stomach!
3:00-5:00 p.m. Next, go to Hardywood Park Craft Brewery. They have a great outdoor space and host food trucks, bands, and other events most evenings.
5:00-6:00 p.m. Have a tasting at Isley Brewing Company. Located in an up-and-coming warehouse district, you will love what they have to offer.
6:00-7:30 p.m. A short distance from Isley is Ardent Craft Ales. This brewery also has a great outdoor space for socializing.
7:30-9:00 p.m. Finish the day with dinner at Burger Bach. This is a New Zealand themed burger pub with delicious food!
